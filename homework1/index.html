Charlize Theron

American actress and produser

Born 7 August 1975 is a South African and American actress and producer.One of the world's highest-paid actresses, Theron is the recipient of numerous accolades,including an Academy Award,a Screen Actors Guild Award,and a Globe Award.In 2016,Time named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world.
